Brain Hack: Retrofitting the Sixth Sense

A brain hack is identifying a neuronal mechanism that was evolutionary (probably) developed to do one thing, and then exploiting or hijacking this mechanism to do another. In this talk, I will present one exemplary model mechanism, how we hacked it, and the surprising results we found.
